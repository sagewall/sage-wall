About Me

I am a GIS analyst and developer with 15 years of experience. Currently, I develop custom web mapping applications and tools for Jefferson County Colorado. Previously, I’ve created multiple web mapping applications and developed custom tools for visualizing and assessing marine magnetic surveys for archaeological resources for the National Park Service and the Bureau of Offshore Energy Management. I have provided GIS analysis and mapping contributions to multiple biological and environmental assessments. I started my professional career as a GIS Specialist mapping archaeological sites along natural gas pipelines and transmission lines. In graduate school I examined prehistoric landscape use and settlement pattern in southwestern Wyoming using ESRI software.
